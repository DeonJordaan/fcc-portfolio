About

Funny, that you should be reading this paragraph, suspended among all the millions of others floating around this tangled Web that we've woven. Another cluster of words herded together as they are. Except, this one is not like all the other ones. It was written, designed and coded by....me!

I certainly didn't see that coming!

It does seem quite odd, seeing as I found computers quite boring when I first encountered them in my teens. Then 'THE INTERNET' happened. Slowly at first, it began to grow then permeate our daily lives. And before we knew it ended up inside our pockets. As it's impact grew, so did my intrigue. Having spent most of my adult life managing a small business in manufacturing, I decided at the end of 2020 to pursue a career in software development.

Not very original, I know.

Skills

My focus has been front-end development and I have actively searched out resources that would give me a comprehensive and thorough understanding of the technologies involved. It has been genuinely fascinating to see how the software and web applications I use daily work under the hood and I hope to explore more of the backend as my knowledge & understanding grow.

One step at a time.
